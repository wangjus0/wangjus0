## Design Context

### Users
- Primary audiences are recruiters, collaborators, and engineers evaluating Justin's profile and work.
- They arrive with limited time and need to quickly assess credibility, current focus, and practical ways to connect.
- The core job to be done is fast trust formation: establish technical signal, show momentum, and make outreach easy.

### Brand Personality
- Thoughtful, technical, approachable.
- Voice should feel clear, grounded, and confident without sounding performative.
- Emotional goal: create confidence and curiosity, then lower friction to start a conversation.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Clean editorial presentation in light mode.
- Blue-teal accents used intentionally for hierarchy and emphasis.
- Motion is restrained and purposeful, supporting readability and orientation rather than decoration.
- Anti-reference: avoid a flashy startup landing page aesthetic.

### Design Principles
- Signal first: prioritize concrete credibility markers and current work before decorative elements.
- Time-respectful scanning: optimize hierarchy so value is clear within seconds.
- Editorial clarity: maintain generous spacing, strong typography rhythm, and concise copy.
- Human technicality: balance rigor with warmth so content feels both competent and approachable.
- Calm interaction: use subtle accents and restrained motion to reinforce focus, not distract from substance.
